    Most NHS treatment is free, but there are a number of things you may have to pay for. This information tells you what help is available, and whether you might get it.

    NHS costs for which you can get help

    You may be able to get help with:-

    • prescription charges
    • NHS dental charges, including check-ups
    • sight tests
    • vouchers towards the cost of glasses and contact lenses
    • travel costs to and from hospital for NHS treatment
    • travel costs if travelling abroad for treatment
    • wigs and fabric supports, for example, abdominal and spinal supports, and support tights.


    Getting the help you're entitled to

    You can get help for lots of different reasons based on your personal circumstances.
    Each reason in which you get help are explained in more detail in the sections on the right, or you can use our checker to help you find out if you get help.
