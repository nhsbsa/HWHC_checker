Help paying NHS costs

Aged 60 and over

People aged 60 and over get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS sight tests
  • and
  • NHS dental check-ups, if you live in Scotland or Wales.

If you get Pension Credit Guarantee Credit

Pension Credit has two parts – Guarantee Pension Credit and Savings Pension Credit.

Guarantee Credit tops up your weekly income if you have a low income below £155.60 (for single people) or £237.55 (for couples)

If you get Pension Credit Guarantee Credit, you get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment for any course of treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You also:

  • Get help towards the cost of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help towards the cost of repair or replacement of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help with travel costs for NHS treatment.
