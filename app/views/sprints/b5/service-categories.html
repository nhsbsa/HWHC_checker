NHSBSA

Help paying for your NHS costs

Most NHS treatment is free, but there are a number of things you may have to pay for.
This information tells you what help is available, if you could get it and how to apply for it.

Prescriptions

Find out if you can get free prescriptions or save money on them

Dental check-ups and treatment

Find out if you can get help to pay for dental check ups and treatment costs

Sight tests, glasses and contact lenses

Find out if you can get help to pay for sight tests and new or replacement glasses and contact lenses

Travel costs for NHS treatment or tests

Find out if you can get help to pay for travel costs to and from hospital or a specialist for further NHS treatment

Wigs and fabric supports

Find out if you can get help to pay for wigs and fabric supports, for example, abdominal and spinal supports, and support tights.

Hearing tests and aids

Find out about free NHS hearing tests, hearing aids and replacement hearing aid batteries.
