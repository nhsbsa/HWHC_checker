You get free prescriptions, but need a valid maternity exemption certificate

If you do not have a valid Maternity Exemption Card you are not automatically exempt from prescription charges.

You can get an application form from your doctor, midwife or health visitor.

If you did not get your Maternity Exemption Card while you were pregnant, you can still apply at any time during the 12 months after your baby is born, this also includes stillborn babies.
