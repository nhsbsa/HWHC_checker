You can't get free help, but could still save money on your precriptions

If you often get more than one prescription a month, for 3 months or more, you could save money with a Prescription Prepayment Certificate.

  • A three month certificate is £29.10
    It could save you money if you need more than 3 prescribed items in 3 months.
  • A yearly certificate is £104.00
    It could save you money if you need more than 12 prescribed items in a year.

    • To spread the cost, yearly Prescription Prepayment Certificates can be paid for by 10 monthly direct debit instalments of £10.40.
