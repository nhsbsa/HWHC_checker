Where to find your NHS number

If you're registered with a GP, you will have a 10 digit NHS number.
It is split into 3 blocks of 3 or 4 numbers, like 123 456 7891.

Your NHS number is on any letter or document you have received from the NHS, such as:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • appointment letters
  • maternity notes
  • on your NHS medical card

Otherwise, contact your GP practice and ask them to look it up for you. They may ask to see your ID, such as a passport or drivers licence.
