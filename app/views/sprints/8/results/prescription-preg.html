You get free prescriptions, but need a valid Maternity Exemption Certificate

NHS prescriptions are free of charge, as long as you have an exemption card.

If you do not have an exemption card you are not automatically exempt from prescription charges.


What you'll need as proof

You may have to show your Maternity Exemption Certificate.

You can get an application form from your midwife, doctor or health visitor.

If you did not get your Maternity Exemption Certificate while you were pregnant, you can still apply at any time during the 12 months after your baby is born, this includes stillborn babies.
