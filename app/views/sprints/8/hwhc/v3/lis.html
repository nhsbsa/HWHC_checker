The low income scheme

If you need to apply for help

You could get help with your health costs if, after your housing costs, you, your partner, or you both have less than:

  • £16,000 in savings, investments or properties – not including where you live
  • £23,250 in savings, investments or properties if you permanently live in a care home

You could get help towards your, your partner’s and your children’s:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
  • travel to receive NHS treatment if you were referred by a GP, hospital doctor, dentist or optician

You can apply at any time. Do not wait until you need treatment.
