Help for people aged 19

If you live in Scotland

If you are aged 19 and you live in Scotland, you are entitled to:

  • Free NHS prescriptions
  • Free sight test
  • Free NHS dental check ups

If you live in Wales

If you are aged 19 and you live in Wales, you are entitled to:

  • Free NHS prescriptions
  • Free NHS dental check ups

Those in full time education

If you are aged 19 and in full time education, you do not get help with health costs, unless:

  • Your parents or guardian have a Tax Credits Exemption Certificate and are receiving a Child Tax Credit payment for you.
  • Your parents or guardian are in receipt of:

    • Income Support,
    Universal Credit,
    Income based Job Seekers Allowance,
    Income related Employment and Support Allowance, or
    Pension Credit Guarantee Credit.
