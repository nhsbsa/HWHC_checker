Help for people aged 60 and over

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS sight tests
  • and
  • NHS dental check-ups, if you live in Scotland or Wales.

If you get Pension Credit Guarantee Credit

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment for any course of treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You also:

  • Get help towards the cost of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help towards the cost of repair or replacement of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help with travel costs for NHS treatment.

You could still get help with your health costs

You do not have to get benefits to get help. If after your housing costs, you, your partner, or you both have less than:

  • £16,000 in savings, investments or properties – not including where you live
  • £23,250 in savings, investments or properties if you permanently live in a care home
