Help for people who are pregnant or have had a baby in the last twelve months

Pregnant women and those who have had a baby in the last 12 months qualify for a Maternity Exemption Certificate.

You must have a valid maternity exemption certificate to get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment if, when you are accepted for a course of treatment, you are pregnant or have had a baby in the previous 12 months.

You can get an application form for a Maternity Exemption Certificate from your doctor, midwife or health visitor.
