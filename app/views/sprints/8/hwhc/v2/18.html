Help for people aged 16, 17 or 18

Those not in full time education

If you are aged 16, 17 or 18 and you are not in full-time education, you are entitled to:

  • Free NHS dental treatment for any course of treatment that starts before your 18th birthday

Help me work out if I can get help with health costs

Those in full time education

If you are aged 16, 17 or 18 and you are in full-time education, you are entitled to:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment for any course of treatment that starts before your 18th birthday
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You also:

  • Get help towards the cost of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help towards the cost of repair or replacement of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help with travel costs for NHS treatment.
