Help for people with a long term medical condition

People with certain medical conditions may give free NHS prescriptions.

  • Diabetes
    Where treatment is not by diet alone
  • Cancer and are undergoing treatment
    Including treatment for the effects of cancer or the effects of cancer treatment.
  • Epilepsy
    Which needs anti-epileptic drugs (AEDs)
  • A continuing physical disability
    Which means you cannot go out without the help of another person
  • You need a continuous surgical dressing or an appliance
    Like a tracheotomy or need to use a stoma
  • An underactive thyroid (hypothyroidism)
    Which needs hormone replacement
If you have a illness that is not listed or you unsure about the name of your condition, check with your doctor, who can advice you about free prescriptions.
