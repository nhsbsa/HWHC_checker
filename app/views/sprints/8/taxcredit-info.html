You get full help with health costs

Anyone named on a {{tctype}} tax credits claim gets free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

They also:

  • Get help towards the cost of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help towards the cost of repair or replacement of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help with travel costs for NHS treatment

You or your parents, should have been sent an NHS Tax Credit Exemption Certificate automatically, but if not, you can show the tax credit award notice as evidence to your health care provider.
