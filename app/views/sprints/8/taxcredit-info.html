You get full help with health costs

Anyone who gets {{tctype}} gets free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

They'll also get help towards the cost of:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing their glasses or contact lenses
  • travelling for NHS treatment

What you'll need as proof

Your exemption card should have been sent automatically.
If you've lost it, you can use the tax credit award notice as proof.
