Find out if you can get help to pay NHS costs


Although most NHS treatments are free, there may still be some costs, for example paying for your prescriptions or dental treatment.

There are lots of different ways to get help paying for NHS costs, depending on your circumstances.

Use this checker to see what help is available and how to get it.

It takes about 5 minutes to use.

This checker is only a guide. You'll still have to apply to find out for certain if you're eligible and how much help you'll get.
