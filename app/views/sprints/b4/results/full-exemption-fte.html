You get help with health costs

Because you're 16 {{age}} and in full time education, you get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS Sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You'll also get help towards the cost of:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses

    • You don't automatically get help towards the cost of:

    • travelling for NHS treatment

    What you should do next

    In most circumstances your date of birth will already be on your health records, but you may have to show proof that you are in full time education, like a recent letter from school or college.

    Prescriptions

    To get free prescriptions tick box B (second box down) on the back of the prescription form.
