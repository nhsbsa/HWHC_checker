You get free prescriptions for your accepted disablement

Because you get a War Pension you get free:

  • NHS prescriptions for your accepted disablement

What you should do next

To get free prescriptions, you need a War Pension Prescription Exemption Certificate.

The certificate means you will not have to pay for your prescribed medication, aids, appliances or equipment required for your accepted disablements.

Contact Veterans UK helpline to apply.


You don't automatically get help towards the cost of:

  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • New or replacement glasses or contact lenses
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
  • Travel to receive NHS treatment

If you find it hard to pay for any of these health costs, you could get help through the Low Income Scheme
