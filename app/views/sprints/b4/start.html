Check what help you could get to pay for NHS costs


Although most NHS treatments are free, you may still have to pay for some things like prescriptions, going to the dentist or opticians.

Check if you are eligible for help with:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs
  • fabric supports, things like surgical bras, support tights and spinal supports.
  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses
  • travelling for NHS treatment

It should take around 5 minutes.
