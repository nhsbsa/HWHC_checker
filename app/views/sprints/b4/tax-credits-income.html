What is a family income?

This is the income that HMRC used to work out your Tax Credit award for the last tax year.

It is made up of either:

  • you and your partner's income, or
  • your parent or guardian's income

If you are unsure, you can check it on your current tax credit award notice or contact the Tax Credit Helpline.
