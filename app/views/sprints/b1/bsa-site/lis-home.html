NHS Low Income Scheme

If you're on a low income, this scheme could you help you pay for all or some of your health costs.

How much help you get depends on your household income and outgoings.

Any help you're entitled to is also available to your partner.

    You could get financial help towards:
  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • Sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • Travel to NHS treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

If you've already paid for treatment, you can apply for a refund at the same time as you apply for a certificate.

More information

NHS help with health cost booklets give you more information about the different ways you can get financial help. Have a look at the guide for the country you live in:

  • England ‒ HC11 help with health costs
  • Scotland ‒ HCS1 help with health costs
