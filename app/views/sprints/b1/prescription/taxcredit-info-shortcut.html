You get full help with health costs

Because you are named on a NHS Tax Credit Exemption Certificate you, your partner and any young people under 20 included on your Tax Credit claim get free:

  • NHS Prescriptions,
  • NHS Dental treatment,
  • Sight tests,
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports.

You'll also:

  • Get help towards the cost of glasses or contact lenses,
  • May get help towards the cost of repair or replacement of glasses or contact lenses,
  • May get help with travel costs for NHS treatment.

    • You may have to show your health care professional your NHS Tax Credit Exemption Certificate.
