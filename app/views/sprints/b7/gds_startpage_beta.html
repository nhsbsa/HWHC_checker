Apply for help to pay for NHS costs

  • Check if you automatically get help to pay for NHS costs

If you have little money to live off, help is available to pay for things like prescriptons, dental treatment and glasses.

The actual amount you get depends on your income, personal circumstances and outgoings, like council tax and housing costs.

If you qualify you, your partner and your children may get help to pay for:

  • NHS dental check-ups and treatments
  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • travel to hospital to receive NHS treatment

Are you already entitled to help?

You can already claim help with health costs if you have an NHS Tax Credit Exemption Certificate or get any of these benefits:
