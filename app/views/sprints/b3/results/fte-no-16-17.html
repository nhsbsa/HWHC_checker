You get free dental care

Because you're {{16}} and not in full time education or training, you get free:

  • NHS dental treatment

You don't automatically get help towards the cost of:

  • Travel to receive NHS treatment

Who can get help

You may get help if:

  • you make an extra journey to receive NHS care after being referred by a doctor, optician or dentist
  • you're named on your parent's qualifying benefit claim or their Low Income Scheme HC2/ HC3 certificate
    • Full list of qualifying benefits

    • Income support
    • Universal credit
    • Income based job seekers allowance
    • Income related employment & support allowance

You get full help with health costs

Because you're under 16, you get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS Sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You'll also get help towards the cost of:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses

    • You don't automatically get help towards the cost of:

    • travelling for NHS treatment
