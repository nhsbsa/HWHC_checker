You get free prescriptions and dental care

Because you're pregnant or have had a baby in the last 12 months, you get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment

What you'll need as proof

Prescriptions

You need to have a valid maternity exemption certificate.

You can get an application form from your midwife, doctor or health visitor.

If you didn't get your maternity exemption certificate while you were pregnant, you can still apply at any time during the 12 months after your baby was born.

Loss of a baby

If you've lost your baby after the 24th week of you pregnancy, you can still apply for a maternity exemption certificate.

If you have a miscarriage, abortion or stillborn birth you can use your maternity exemption certificate until it expires.
