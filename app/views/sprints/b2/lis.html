    1. Buy a prescription prepayment certificate

    • NHS prescriptions

    You can buy prescription prepayment certificates (PPCs) from the NHS which may make your prescriptions cheaper.

    A 3 or 12 month prescription prepayment covers all your prescriptions for that period, no matter how many you need.

    A prescription costs £8.40 per item, but a prescription prepayment costs:

    • £29.10 for 3 months
    • £104 for 12 months

    This means if you’re going to buy 4 or more prescriptions in 3 months, or 13 or more prescriptions in 12 months, it may be cheaper to buy a prescription prepayment.
