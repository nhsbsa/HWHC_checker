What’s a diabetes review?

Your GP will go over your blood test results and discuss your:

  • blood sugar levels from the past 12 months and set a target with you for the next 3 months
  • blood fats (cholesterol) level
  • liver function

The review is also a general health check. You’ll have:

  • your blood pressure taken
  • a urine test for kidney function
  • your weight measured
